Week 2 : Data Cleaning and Visualization
Objective: Clean the Pakistan Job Market dataset and create 10 visualizations that tell a story about Pakistan's job market.
Dataset: Pakistan Job Market Intelligence Dataset, 10500 rows, 13 columns.
What was done:
Wrote a clean_data() function that handles missing values, removes duplicates, converts dates to datetime, and creates 2 new features (Posting Year and is_Remote).
Created 10 charts including time series, grouped bar, heatmap, regional bar, boxplot, scatter with regression, histogram with KDE, pie chart, job titles bar, and education level bar.
Key Findings:
Karachi has the most jobs with 2100+ postings. Most jobs pay between 70k to 100k PKR. 64.6% of jobs are onsite, only 16.8% are remote. Bachelors degree is required in most job postings. Product Manager is the most in demand job title. March 2025 had the highest job postings at 948.
