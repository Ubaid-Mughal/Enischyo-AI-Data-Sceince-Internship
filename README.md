Enischyo AI and Data Science Internship
This repository contains all weekly project submissions for the Enischyo AI and Data Science Internship program.
Week 1 : Pakistan Job Market EDA
Objective: Explore and analyze the Pakistan Job Market Intelligence Dataset to identify data quality issues and extract meaningful insights.
Dataset: Pakistan Job Market Intelligence Dataset from Kaggle, 10500 rows and 13 columns covering May 2024 to May 2025.
Key Findings: Karachi has the most job postings. IT and Technology is the most active sector. Islamabad has the highest average salaries. Only 17 percent of jobs are remote. City column has spelling inconsistencies that need cleaning in Week 2.
